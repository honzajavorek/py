Meetups (Pyvo)

Python meetups (called “Pyvo”) are happening across the country. You're welcome to come, or present at one!

Please mind some local specifics

  • Meetups are in Czech language by default, because most of Czechs are still uncomfortable to speak in English publicly. It's OK for you to speak and present in English, though. Many people will be also very happy to have English discussions over a beer with you.
  • “Pivo” means “beer” in Czech. Meetups are usually very informal, held in pubs.
  • Every event usually selects a topic in advance and people volunteer to give talks on that topic.
  • It's perfectly fine to walk in and give a lightning talk about any topic at the end of the event.
  • Pyvo events aim to be welcoming, friendly environment. Read our Code of Conduct.
